Matthew Lei

Hello, my name is Matthew Lei, and I am a Georgia Tech first-year Computer Science major from Duluth, Georgia.

I want to join Bits of Good because not only will it help me further my skills in web development, but it will also allow me to contribute to the community.

While I empathize with many moves for social change, the main thing I am passionate about is bringing computer science to those with less exposure to it.

When I was in elementary school, my dad took me to computer science bootcamps that taught me Scratch.

Those activities jumpstarted my interest in the field, and I have learned much since while still having much left to do.

As a result, I want the next generations to have the same opportunities as I did so that they may become interested to explore computer science.
